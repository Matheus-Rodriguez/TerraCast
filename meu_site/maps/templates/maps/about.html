About TerraCast

Predict today to protect tomorrow.

TerraCast is a predictive intelligence platform specialized in anticipating armed conflicts and geopolitical instabilities on a global scale. Using AI technologies, statistical modeling, and machine learning, we transform complex data into actionable strategic insights.

Our system analyzes thousands of indicators — from economic, social, and political data to environmental and behavioral variables — to identify risk patterns and forecast conflict scenarios up to 12 months in advance. These predictions assist governments, organizations, and humanitarian agencies in making informed decisions, reducing uncertainties, and saving lives.

Our Mission

To anticipate crises before they become tragedies and provide data-driven intelligence for a safer, transparent world.

Our Vision

To be the world’s leading reference in predictive geopolitical risk technology, contributing to conflict prevention and sustainable peace strategies.

Our Differentials

  • Proprietary AI models calibrated with historical data and updated.
  • Global coverage, with subnational forecasts and interactive risk maps.
  • Multidisciplinary team in data science, politics, and security.
  • Integration with open sources, financial indexes, media, and generative AI for automated reports.
